Selection of the appropriate Army Technical Manuals varies by basic series and stock classifications as well as the intended audience. For example, in TM 55-1680-316-10, the 55 signifies transportation, while the 1680 signifies miscellaneous aircraft accessories and components. The 316 is a series identifier and the 10 indicates the intended audience is operator or crew maintenance. Likewise, in the TM 11-5965-285-23, the 11 indicates this is a publication on signals, the 5965 relates to headsets and the 23 indicates this manual is for maintainer maintenance use.
